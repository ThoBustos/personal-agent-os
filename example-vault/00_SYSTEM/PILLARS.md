# The 10 Life Pillars

These are not "areas of interest." They are **life systems**. If one collapses, others eventually follow.

---

## 1. Body & Energy

Not optimization. **Reliability.**

**Includes:** Strength, mobility, cardio, sleep, nutrition, nervous system, recovery, play.

**Monthly lens:** Is my body becoming more powerful, more resilient, more expressive?

#pillar/body

---

## 2. Spirit & Meaning

Not religion. **Orientation.**

**Includes:** Stillness, awe, gratitude, mortality awareness, nature, surrender, inner honesty.

**Monthly lens:** Do I feel connected to existence, or am I just executing tasks?

#pillar/spirit

---

## 3. Mind & Clarity

This is about **signal vs noise**.

**Includes:** Thinking time, reading, writing, learning, mental hygiene, reducing clutter.

**Monthly lens:** Is my thinking getting clearer, simpler, and more strategic?

#pillar/mind

---

## 4. Love & Relationships

**Includes:** Romantic relationship, family, deep friendships, emotional mastery, presence.

**Monthly lens:** Are my relationships deepening, or just continuing?

#pillar/relationships

---

## 5. Self-Time & Joy

This is where **aliveness** comes from.

**Includes:** Solitude, play, exploration, beauty, art, pleasure without productivity.

**Monthly lens:** When was the last time I felt childlike, curious, or enchanted?

#pillar/joy

---

## 6. Creation & Craft

This is not about money. It's about **expression**.

**Includes:** Building, writing, teaching, designing, problem-solving, mastery.

**Monthly lens:** What am I building that didn't exist before me?

#pillar/creation

---

## 7. Wealth & Leverage

Money is not identity. It's **optionality**.

**Includes:** Income streams, ownership, scalability, investments, runway.

**Monthly lens:** Is my life becoming more free, or more fragile?

#pillar/wealth

---

## 8. Impact & Service

This is not charity. It's **legacy**.

**Includes:** Who you help, what you enable, what you make easier for others.

**Monthly lens:** If I disappeared, what would be missing?

#pillar/impact

---

## 9. Media & Personal Brand

This is how your mind **scales**.

**Includes:** Writing, speaking, podcasting, teaching publicly, distribution.

**Monthly lens:** Is my worldview being expressed, or trapped in my head?

#pillar/media

---

## Daily Compass

Every day, check:
- [ ] Body - Did I move?
- [ ] Create - Did I make something?
- [ ] Truth - Did I tell the truth?
- [ ] Connect - Did I connect with someone?
- [ ] Awe - Did I feel gratitude or wonder?

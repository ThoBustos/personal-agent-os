# 01_GOALS Guide

This folder contains goals at different time horizons, cascading from life direction down to quarterly focus.

## Files

| File | Purpose | Update Frequency |
|------|---------|------------------|
| `Life.md` | Long-term direction (10yr, 5yr) | Yearly |
| `{{YEAR}}.md` | This year's milestones | Quarterly review |
| `{{YEAR}}-Q#.md` | This quarter's focus | Monthly review |

## How Goals Cascade

```
LIFE_VISION.md (North Star Identity)
    ↓
Life.md (10yr, 5yr milestones)
    ↓
Year.md (This year)
    ↓
Quarter.md (This quarter)
    ↓
Weekly (This week)
    ↓
Daily (Today)
```

## For Claude

When helping with goals:
1. Always connect back to `LIFE_VISION.md` for alignment
2. Check `PILLARS.md` for balance across life areas
3. Goals should be identity-based, not just outcome-based
